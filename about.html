Founder's Message

Every child I met changed me.

"I spent 12 years as a school counselor watching brilliant children fail — not because they lacked intelligence, but because we were measuring the wrong things. A future architect labeled 'slow' in math. A future musician told music was a hobby.

When I discovered DMIT science, everything changed. For the first time, I could show parents — with data — exactly how their child's brain was wired. I could say: 'This child isn't failing. This child is being taught the wrong way.'

MindDecode was born from that moment. We exist to make sure no child grows up not knowing their own genius."

"Intelligence is not a single river — it's a delta. DMIT finds YOUR unique channel."
